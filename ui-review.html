Change 1 of 12

Deeper Surface Hierarchy

Current: flat gray cards (#2C2C2E) on gray background (#1C1C1E) — only 6% lightness difference. Proposed: blue-black base (#0D0D12) with cards at #14141C. This creates stronger depth perception and follows the Material Design 3 principle of "elevated = lighter." The blue-black undertone feels more premium than neutral gray.

Change 2 of 12

Sidebar Navigation (Desktop)

Current: horizontal top bar eats vertical space and crowds nav items + CTA into one line. Proposed: 220px collapsible sidebar gives room for clear labels, active state indicator (accent left bar), and a prominent "Start Standup" CTA pinned at bottom. This is the dominant 2025-26 SaaS pattern (Linear, Raycast, Notion). Mobile stays as bottom tabs.

Change 3 of 12

Stats Cards with Subtle Glow + Trends

Current: generic cards with big bold numbers but no context. You can't tell if "7 day streak" is good or bad. Proposed: add trend indicators, uppercase label style, subtle radial glow on the accent card, and tighter spacing. The glow draws the eye to the most important metric.

Change 7 of 12

Buttons with Glow + Press Feedback

Current: flat color buttons with simple hover color change. Proposed: primary buttons get a subtle ambient glow in the accent color, slight lift on hover (translateY -1px), and scale-down on press (0.97). Secondary and ghost buttons get subtle background fills on hover. Makes the UI feel more tactile and responsive.

Change 8 of 12

Typography — Softer Weights, Better Spacing

Current: heavy bold (700) on page titles, pure white (#fff), tight letter-spacing. In dark mode, bold white text causes visual fatigue and "halation" (text bleeding). Proposed: semibold (600) max for headings, off-white (#F0F0F5), slight negative tracking on headings, positive tracking on body. Text colors from slate palette (#94A3B8, #64748B) instead of raw grays (#888, #555).

Change 10 of 12

Skeleton Loading States

Current: just the text "Loading..." in gray. Proposed: shimmer skeleton screens that match the layout shape of what's loading. Feels faster, reduces perceived wait time, and prevents layout shifts. Every modern SaaS uses these.

Change 11 of 12

Onboarding Progress — Step Dots

Current: text-based "3 of 5" with a thin progress bar. The percentage text is redundant with the bar. Proposed: segmented step dots — each step is a separate segment that fills as you complete it. The active segment gets a subtle glow. Cleaner, more spatial, and shows exactly how many steps remain.

Change 12 of 12

Empty States — More Inviting

Current: small icon, muted text, no CTA. Empty states are the first thing new users see. Proposed: larger icon with subtle border ring, stronger heading text, ambient glow behind the icon, and a clear CTA button. Turns a dead end into an invitation.
